Append text at the beginning of each line via sed_ stream editor.

::

  Append ``| `` to the beginning of each line.

.. code-block:: bash

  sed 's/^/| /' tmp.txt > tmp2.txt


----

Tested on ``Ubuntu Linux 15.10``, ``sed 4.2.2-6.1``.
